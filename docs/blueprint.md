# **App Name**: IndoJobLink

## Core Features:

- KTP Verification: Verify the identity of daily workers using KTP (ID card) verification.
- Job Dashboard: Provide a dashboard for job seekers to browse and find available jobs.
- Go Online - Instant Matching: Enable real-time matching between job seekers and employers when they are both online.
- Job Details: Display complete job details, including requirements and location.
- Completion Verification: Allow employers to verify the completion of the job by the worker.
- Automated Payments: Enable automated payments to workers upon job completion.
- AI Candidate Recommendation: Suggest suitable candidates based on job requirements for employers with a subscription.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep orange (#FF851B) to convey energy and dynamism, referencing the vibrant culture of Indonesia and contrasting with a light background.
- Background color: Very light orange (#FFF3E0).
- Accent color: Yellow-orange (#FFAC33) to highlight interactive elements and important CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern and approachable look.
- Use clean, modern icons representing job categories and actions.
- Clean and intuitive layout, prioritizing ease of navigation for all user types.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience.
